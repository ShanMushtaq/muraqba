Payment Terms

MURAQBA operates on a results-first model. The client pays a 30% advance upfront, and the remaining 70% is due only after the agreed milestone is achieved and demonstrated to the client.
  • Advance (30%): PKR __________ — due before work begins. Covers setup and onboarding costs.
  • Remaining (70%): PKR __________ — due within 3 days of milestone being achieved.
  • Ad Budget: The client's monthly ad budget (minimum PKR 5,000) is separate from the agency fee and is paid directly to Meta by the client.
  • Late payment: Payments delayed beyond 5 days after milestone achievement may result in work being paused.
  • Currency: All fees are in Pakistani Rupees (PKR) unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Refund Policy

MURAQBA takes client satisfaction seriously. The following refund terms apply:

  • Within 3 days of campaign launch: If the client is unsatisfied for any reasonable cause, the 30% advance is fully refundable.
  • After 3 days: No refunds are issued. However, MURAQBA commits to continuing work until the agreed milestone is reached before collecting remaining payment.
  • No refund will be issued if the client fails to provide required assets, approvals, or access within agreed timelines, causing delays.
4

Revision Policy

  • Ad Campaigns: Ad creatives, targeting, and campaign decisions are managed by MURAQBA professionally. Client revisions to ad campaigns are not included.
  • Content Creation: Each client receives 3 free revision rounds per month for posts, captions, and creatives.
  • Additional revisions beyond 3 rounds are charged at PKR 500 per revision request.
  • Revision requests must be made within 24 hours of content delivery. Approved content will be published as-is after 24 hours.
5

Ad Account & Asset Ownership

The client retains full ownership of their Facebook page, Instagram account, Meta Ads account, and all brand assets at all times.
  • Client's accounts: All social media pages, ad accounts, and brand assets belong to the client. MURAQBA is granted temporary admin/manager access to manage campaigns on the client's behalf.
  • Access removal: The client may remove MURAQBA's access at any time, which will be treated as contract termination.
  • Content created: Original content created by MURAQBA for the client becomes the client's property upon full payment.
  • MURAQBA's strategies and templates remain the intellectual property of MURAQBA and may not be resold or distributed.
  • Ad Spend: Money spent on ads is managed by the client's own budget. MURAQBA holds no financial liability for ad spend results beyond professional management.
